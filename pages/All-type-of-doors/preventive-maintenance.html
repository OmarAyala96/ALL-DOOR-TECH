Preventive Maintenance Programs

A proactive approach is the best way to avoid costly downtime and safety hazards. At All Door Tech, our preventive maintenance services are designed to keep your commercial and industrial doors operating at peak efficiency while extending their operational lifespan.

We provide scheduled inspections that cover everything from lubrication and balance to structural integrity, ensuring your facility remains secure and compliant with all local safety regulations in Riverside, CA.

Maximize Uptime & Reduce Costs

Minor issues can quickly escalate into expensive emergency repairs. Our custom maintenance plans identify wear and tear early, preventing sudden failures in high-traffic areas such as loading docks, warehouses, and storefront entrances.

Proactive Care for Every Door

Our technicians use a comprehensive checklist to ensure every mechanical and electrical component of your door system is functioning perfectly.

Multi-Point Inspection

Detailed evaluation of springs, cables, rollers, and hinges to detect signs of fatigue before they cause a system failure.

Safety Sensor Testing

Testing of photo-eyes and auto-reverse mechanisms to ensure full compliance with ADA and commercial safety standards.

Precision Lubrication

Application of industrial-grade lubricants to moving parts to reduce friction, noise, and mechanical wear.

Safety & Compliance Services

Comprehensive maintenance solutions to ensure your commercial doors meet industry regulations and operational safety standards.

AAADM Inspections

Certified annual inspections for automatic doors to guarantee compliance with ANSI standards, reducing liability and ensuring pedestrian safety in public entrances.

Fire Door Testing

Mandatory annual drop testing and certification for fire-rated doors to ensure they close properly during an emergency, protecting your property and personnel.

Asset Management

We provide detailed digital logs and tagging for every door in your facility, making it easy to track service history, budget for upgrades, and manage large-scale repairs.

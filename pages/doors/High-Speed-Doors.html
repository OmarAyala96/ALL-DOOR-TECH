High Speed Doors

Advanced rapid-roll solutions designed for durability, security, and climate control in high-traffic environments.

Overview Specifications Why Choose Us

Industrial Speed & Efficiency

Our high-speed doors are engineered to handle the intense demands of industrial facilities, offering opening speeds of up to 100 inches per second.

By minimizing the time a door remains open, you significantly reduce energy loss, improve worker comfort, and prevent the entry of dust or debris into sensitive areas.

Technical Specifications

  • Safety Systems: Integrated infrared light curtains and bottom-edge wireless sensors for advanced obstacle detection.
  • Operating Speed: Opening speeds up to 100 inches per second (2.5 m/s) to ensure seamless forklift traffic.
  • Durability: Self-repairing "break-away" technology that allows the curtain to reset after accidental impact.
  • Environmental Control: Superior seal technology to maintain temperature zones, prevent dust infiltration, and reduce energy costs in cold storage or cleanroom applications.

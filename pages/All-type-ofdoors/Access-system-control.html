Classic Access Security Solutions

Keypad Entry Systems represent the most reliable and traditional method for managing access. Widely implemented across retail, corporate, and industrial sectors, these systems grant entry via a personalized security code.

Advanced engineering allows ALLDOORTECH devices to interface seamlessly with modern hardware, including magnetic locks, electric strikes, and automated gate operators. While often used as standalone units, they can also be integrated into complex security networks.

Simple, keyless security designed for high-traffic commercial environments.

Professional Keypad Devices

ALLDOORTECH offers a broad selection of high-performance keypad devices tailored to your facility's specific security needs:

  • Standalone Units: Build-in touchlock systems with optional Bluetooth access for smartphone control and audit trails.
  • Integrated Digital Keypads: Linked to a central system to monitor real-time movement and unique user codes.
  • Outdoor/Weatherproof: Durable, protected units capable of operating reliably for years in harsh weather conditions.
  • Dormakaba Solutions: Premium push-button locks for robust mechanical and electronic security.
